Our Story

CAteriNgz began in a small kitchen and has grown into a renowned catering service in Jakarta, Bandung, and Malang. Our mission is to create unforgettable culinary experiences using the finest ingredients.

Mission & Values

We are dedicated to sustainable practices and supporting local farmers and artisans to ensure top-notch quality in every meal. Our commitment to quality means we use fresh, locally-sourced ingredients to bring out the best flavors in every dish. By working closely with local producers, we not only guarantee exceptional taste but also contribute to the community's well-being and environmental sustainability.
